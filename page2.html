Population of Pakistan

Pakistan ki abaadi taqreeban 240 million (24 crore) hai, jo ise duniya ka paanchwan sabse bara mulk banati hai. Aabaadi ka bara hissa nojawanon par mushtamil hai, jahan 64% logon ki umar 30 saal se kam hai. Mulk ki abaadi ka 36.4% shehri ilaqon mein rehta hai, jabke 63.6% dehati ilaqon mein. Pakistan ke chaar bare صوبے hain: Punjab, Sindh, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, aur Balochistan, jinmein Punjab sabse zyada populated hai. Urdu qaumi zuban hai, lekin Punjabi, Sindhi, Pashto, aur Balochi bhi aam hai. Mulk ki 96% abaadi Muslim hai, magar Hindu, Christian, aur Ahmadiyya minorities bhi paayi jaati hain. Taleemi shara 60% ke qareeb hai, jo behtri ki taraf ja rahi hai lekin ab bhi qabil-e-tawajju hai.
Pakistan mein abaadi 2% ke annual growth rate se barh rahi hai, jo mulk ke liye chunaouti hai. Barhti hui abaadi se resources aur infrastructure par bohot zyada dabao hai, jahan taleem, sehat aur safai ki sahoolatein muqabil nahi hain. Iqtisadiyat bhi abaadi ke barhne se mutasir ho rahi hai, jahan rozgaar aur waastay ke moqay kam hain. Yeh zaroori hai ke abaadi ke masail ko dour karne ke liye munasib hikmat-e-amli ikhtiyar ki jaye, taki mulk taraqqi ki raah par gaamzan ho sake aur awaam ke zindagi ke mayaar mein behtri aaye.
