Beautifull area of Pakistan

1. Hunza Valley

Located in the Gilgit-Baltistan region, Hunza Valley is famous for its breathtaking scenery, including the Rakaposhi and Ultar Sar mountains. The valley is home to charming villages, crystal-clear rivers, and lush green fields.

2. Skardu

Skardu, also in Gilgit-Baltistan, is the gateway to some of the world's highest peaks, including K2. The region features stunning landscapes such as the Shangrila Resort (also known as Heaven on Earth), the serene Satpara Lake, and the mesmerizing Deosai National Park.

3.Fairy Meadows

This beautiful area offers a picturesque view of Nanga Parbat, the world's ninth-highest mountain. Fairy Meadows is an idyllic spot for trekkers and nature lovers, providing lush green meadows and a serene atmosphere.

4. Swat Valley

Often referred to as the "Switzerland of the East," Swat Valley is known for its lush green landscapes, rivers, and scenic beauty. The valley includes places like Malam Jabba, Mingora, and Kalam, each offering unique experiences.

5. Neelum Valley

Located in Azad Kashmir, Neelum Valley is famous for its enchanting rivers, dense forests, and vibrant flora. Key attractions include the stunning Shounter Lake, Keran, and Ratti Gali Lake.

6.Murree

A popular hill station near Islamabad, Murree offers cool weather and lush green hills. Key spots include Mall Road, Pindi Point, and the Patriata (New Murree) chairlift.

8. Chitral

Chitral, located in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, is known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. The Kalash Valley, with its unique culture and festivals, and the Chitral Gol National Park are major attractions.
